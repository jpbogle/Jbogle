I'm Jeremy Bogle, a Computer Science and Electrical Engineering major at MIT with a minor in Economics. I love being creative and building and design products. I am currently working at Nasdaq doing web development and product creation. I also enjoy working on a startup with my brother and friend called Up Photography where we take aerial photographs and make custom videos of houses for Real Estate companies. At MIT I'm taking 4 classes and am on both the varsity Swimming and Water Polo teams as well as a member of the professional fraternity, Theta Tau. I am the Technology Chair and the Assistant House Manager for Theta Tau. I am also an being an active member of the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science department and have spent time as a lab assistant in their new design studio.

Being on both the water polo and swim teams and having grown up playing sports, I have always really enjoyed staying active. I generally do not like to do monotonous, uncreative things. In terms of school,this translates and I enjoy problem solving and specifically have enjoyed the puzzles of EE in 6.01 and 6.004. Outside of school, I also work on my startup with my friend where we film high-quality aerial videos and take aerial photos of houses with a drone for advertising for real estate companies in the Boston area. We have done this for two summers and had success making money this past summer and will continue to work on this through the winter and into summer 2016
